About キボ(Kibo)

The name キボ(pronounced kee-boh), carries a deeper meaning than just a cool-sounding word. It draws inspiration from the Japanese word for "hope". We believe technology should be a source of hope, a tool that empowers you to achieve your dreams and explore new possibilities.


Think of it like a spark. Just like hope ignites a fire within you to reach for your goals, Kibo aims to provide the essential tools that help you turn those hopes into realities. Whether you're a creative professional, a passionate gamer, or simply someone who wants to get the most out of your computer, Kibo strives to equip you with the right peripherals to unlock your full potential.


More than just a name, Kibo is a philosophy. We believe in the power of technology to connect you to your passions, fuel your creativity, and empower you to achieve your goals. So, when you choose Kibo, you're not just buying a computer accessory, you're taking a step towards a future filled with hope and possibility.
